NATIOKS is a webapp that allows each of the Palestinians and Israelies to get to know more about the other culture by reading books. NATIOKS connects two different nationalities and empowers the connection between them,Israelis and Palestinians can read the same books. Books can make a social impact and change the people's minds, thoughts and opinions.
